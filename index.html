About Pura Organic

Pura Organic Agro Tech Ltd, incorporated in 2011 and based in Uganda, has established itself as a leader in cassava farming and processing. Over the years, we've expanded our nucleus farm, increased our cassava acreage, and developed our production of cassava cuttings for smallholder farmers.

Today, we're proud to have evolved into a full cassava processing operation, creating value-added products like starch, flour, and tapioca pearls that meet international quality standards.
