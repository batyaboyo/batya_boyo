About AgNect

Born from a vision to transform Ugandan agriculture through technology, AgNect bridges the gap between traditional farming practices and cutting-edge innovations.

Our team of agricultural experts, data scientists, and rural development specialists works directly with farmers to create solutions that address their unique challenges.

We believe in climate-smart agriculture that respects local knowledge while embracing the power of data to create a more sustainable and profitable future for Ugandan farmers.
