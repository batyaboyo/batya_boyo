Our Story

Pura Organic Agro Tech Ltd was incorporated in 2011 in Uganda with a vision to revolutionize cassava farming and processing in East Africa. What began as a small nucleus farm has grown into one of the region's leading agricultural enterprises.

Over the years, we've been dedicated to developing sustainable farming practices, expanding our cassava acreage, and producing high-quality cassava cuttings for smallholder farmers in the community.

Today, we've evolved into a full-scale cassava processing operation, creating value-added products that meet international standards while maintaining our commitment to organic farming practices and community development.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality, organic cassava products while empowering local communities through sustainable agricultural practices and fair trade relationships.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's leading producer of premium cassava products, recognized globally for quality, sustainability, and positive impact on farming communities.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We're committed to farming practices that protect the environment and ensure long-term resource availability.
